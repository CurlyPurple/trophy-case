About Trophy Case

Welcome to Trophy Case! Your personal account to organize and prioritize your goals. You can add goals by naming them, adding a description that further explains what you are trying to accomplish, designating how many days a week you want to attempt this goal, and finally the priority level you want to assign this goal with 1 being the highest priority.
